1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Spectral Scribe collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Collect

Our extension does not collect or store any personal information. It requires access to:

  • The current tab's URL (only for YouTube videos)
  • The content of the current web page (when using the text extraction feature)
  • Your API keys for Anthropic (Claude) and Akash AI services, which are stored locally in your browser's storage

3. How We Use Information

The information accessed by our extension is used solely for providing the extension's functionality. We do not collect, store, or transmit this information to our servers.

4. Data Storage

Your API keys are stored locally in your browser's storage and are not transmitted to us or any third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

Our extension uses the Anthropic (Claude) and Akash AI services to process text and generate responses. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for information on how they handle data.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
